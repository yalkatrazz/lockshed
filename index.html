Built for security. Designed for you.

Every feature in The LockShed is built around one principle: your data never leaves your device.

End-to-end encryption

AES-256-CBC combined with HMAC-SHA256 protects every entry in the vault, locally and without the cloud.

Smart password analysis

Built-in zxcvbn analysis shows how strong each password really is — not just its length.

All your devices

A browser extension with autofill (Ctrl+Shift+L) and a PIN-protected PWA for your phone over your own WiFi.

Auto-lock & history

The vault locks itself automatically when idle, and keeps track of previous passwords for each entry.

Export & import

PDF export for your notes and CSV import for easy migration from other managers.

Themes & scaling

Several visual themes and global font scaling — make the interface your own.

One vault. All your surfaces.

The LockShed runs as a desktop app on Windows, but reaches further: a local Flask server powers a browser extension for quick autofill, and a PIN-protected PWA lets you reach the vault from your phone — all over your own local network, nothing in the cloud.
